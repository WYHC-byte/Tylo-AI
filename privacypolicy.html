AI Memory Feature Guide

What is AI Memory?

Our AI Memory system creates a personalized experience by remembering your preferences, interests, and conversation patterns. This allows TyloAI to provide more relevant and contextual responses tailored specifically to you.

How Memory Works

AI Memory analyzes your conversations to identify and store important information about you, such as:

  • Personal preferences and interests
  • Professional background and expertise areas
  • Communication style preferences
  • Recurring topics and questions
  • Goals and projects you're working on

Remove specific memories or clear all data completely.

AI Personality Analysis

Based on your interactions, we can generate a unique "Personality Card" that shows how AI perceives your characteristics and traits. This feature is:

  • Entertainment Purpose: For fun and self-reflection, not professional assessment
  • Privacy Protected: Generated locally and never shared without consent
  • Shareable: Export as image to share with friends if desired

Privacy Note: All memory data is stored locally in your browser and can be completely deleted at any time. We never use this data for advertising or share it with third parties.
